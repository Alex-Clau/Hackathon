# Company Logos

Place your company logo images in this folder with the following names:

- `nike.png` - Nike logo
- `adidas.png` - Adidas logo
- `zara.png` - Zara logo
- `hm.png` - H&M logo
- `puma.png` - Puma logo

## Image Requirements

- Format: PNG, JPG, or SVG
- Recommended size: 200x200px or larger (square aspect ratio works best)
- The images will be displayed at 128x128px in the mobile app

## Accessing Images

Once uploaded, images will be accessible at:
- `http://localhost:3000/images/companies/techcorp.png`
- `http://YOUR_IP:3000/images/companies/techcorp.png` (for mobile devices)
